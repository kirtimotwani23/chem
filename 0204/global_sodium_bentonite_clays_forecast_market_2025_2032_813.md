<h1>Sodium Bentonite Clays Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Sodium bentonite clay is a naturally occurring material known for its high swelling capacity and water absorption properties. It is primarily composed of montmorillonite and is widely used in industries such as oil and gas, construction, agriculture, and environmental applications.
