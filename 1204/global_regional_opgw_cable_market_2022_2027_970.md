It serves the dual purpose of providing grounding and shielding against lightning strikes while also incorporating fiber optic communication capabilities. This makes OPGW cables crucial in modern power transmission networks, enabling both power utility functions and high-speed data transmission. OPGW cables are commonly deployed in high-voltage transmission systems, ensuring reliability and efficiency in power grid operations.</p><p>
