<strong>Market Size</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Polyacrylonitrile (PAN) Fiber is a type of synthetic fiber primarily produced through the polymerization of acrylonitrile, a petroleum-derived monomer. This fiber is known for its exceptional strength, high thermal stability, resistance to chemicals, and UV rays, making it highly durable in various environments. PAN Fiber is a critical precursor to carbon fiber production, which exhibits superior mechanical properties and thermal conductivity.</p><p>
</p><p>In addition to its role in carbon fiber production, PAN Fiber is widely used across diverse industries, including textiles, filtration, medical, and aerospace. The versatility of PAN Fiber stems from its ability to perform in extreme conditions, offering applications ranging from creating high-performance fabrics to being employed in specialized filtration materials. Due to its importance in these sectors, the PAN Fiber market is gaining prominence, exhibiting steady growth globally.</p><p>
