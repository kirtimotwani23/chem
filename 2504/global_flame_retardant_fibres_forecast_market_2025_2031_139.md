</p><p>Low flammability</p><p>Slow burning speed</p><p>Self-extinguishing capability</p><p>Low smoke emission</p><p>Non-toxic</p><p>
</p><p>These properties make them ideal for critical applications in <strong>protective clothing, upholstery, automotive interiors</strong>, and <strong>aerospace components</strong>.</p><p>
