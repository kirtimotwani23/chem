<h1>Reflective Tape for Workwear Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Reflective tape for workwear is a specialized material incorporated into clothing to increase visibility in low-light conditions. It is designed to reflect light from external sources, such as vehicle headlights, making the wearer highly visible to others. This is especially critical in environments where visibility is a safety concern, including construction zones, roadwork, and emergency services. Reflective tape typically appears as a bright, shiny strip of material made from microprismatic or glass bead technology, which is attached to garments such as safety vests, work jackets, and uniforms.</p><p>
