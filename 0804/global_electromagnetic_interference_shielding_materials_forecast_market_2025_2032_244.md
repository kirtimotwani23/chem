<h1>Electromagnetic Interference Shielding Materials Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Electromagnetic interference (EMI) shielding materials are essential for protecting electronic devices and systems from the disruptive effects of electromagnetic radiation. These materials are used to prevent unwanted electromagnetic signals from interfering with sensitive electronic equipment, ensuring the proper functioning and safety of devices. They are commonly applied in industries such as consumer electronics, communication systems, defense, and aviation. EMI shielding materials work by absorbing or reflecting electromagnetic waves, preventing them from penetrating or escaping from a device or system.</p><p>
