<h1>N-Dodecane Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>N-Dodecane is a straight-chain alkane with the chemical formula C12H26. It is primarily utilized in a variety of industrial and chemical applications due to its unique properties. As a solvent, it serves as an effective solvent for many organic compounds, especially in the field of industrial chemical processes. N-Dodecane is also commonly used as a distillation chaser to eliminate residual solvents, making it a crucial part of the petrochemical and chemical industries.</p><p>
</p><p>Another significant use of N-Dodecane is in reprocessing plants, where it functions as a diluent for tributyl phosphate (TBP) in nuclear fuel reprocessing. It is also explored as a potential alternative to kerosene-based fuels in jet engines due to its comparable chemical properties.
