<strong>Market Size</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Performance polyolefins are advanced polymer materials known for their exceptional durability, flexibility, and lightweight properties. These materials are widely used in industries such as packaging, automotive, construction, and healthcare. Polyolefins, primarily polyethylene (PE) and polypropylene (PP), are engineered to enhance mechanical strength, chemical resistance, and thermal stability, making them ideal for high-performance applications.</p><p>
