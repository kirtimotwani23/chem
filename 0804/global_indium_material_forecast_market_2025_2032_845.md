<h1>Indium Material Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Indium is a rare, silvery-white post-transition metal known for its softness and malleability. It is chemically similar to gallium and tin and is primarily used in high-tech applications, including the production of liquid crystal displays (LCDs), semiconductors, and photovoltaic panels. Indium is extracted as a byproduct of zinc ore processing and has a relatively low melting point, making it highly valuable in industrial applications.</p><p>
