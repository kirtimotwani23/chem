</p><p>Sodium Xylene Sulfonate (SXS) is a hydrotrope used in various industries, including household and industrial cleaning, personal care, and textiles. As a non-toxic and biodegradable surfactant, it helps enhance the solubility of hydrophobic compounds in water-based formulations. The primary role of SXS is to reduce surface tension, improve the spreadability of liquids, and boost the performance of cleaning agents, making it a crucial ingredient in the formulation of detergents, shampoos, and industrial cleaners.</p><p>
