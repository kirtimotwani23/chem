</p><p><strong>Geranyl Linalool (CAS 1113-21-9)</strong> is a naturally occurring diterpene alcohol commonly used for its fragrant properties. In addition to its fragrance, Geranyl Linalool also has insecticidal properties, particularly effective against xylophagous insects that affect pine wood, making it an important compound in pest control.</p><p>
