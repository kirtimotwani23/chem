<h1>Protein A Purification Resin Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Protein A purification resin plays a crucial role in the biopharmaceutical industry. It is a specialized chromatography resin used primarily for the purification of monoclonal antibodies (mAbs) and other immunoglobulins. Protein A binds specifically to the Fc region of immunoglobulin G (IgG), allowing for highly selective purification. This resin is widely utilized in research, diagnostic, and therapeutic applications, including vaccine production, biosimilar manufacturing, and protein-based drug development.</p><p>
