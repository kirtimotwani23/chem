<h1>Selective Fluorine Reagent Market Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Selective Fluorine Reagent (CAS 140681-55-6) is a specialty chemical compound widely used as a fluorinating agent in the synthesis of fluorine-containing compounds. With a molecular formula of C7H14B2ClF9N2, this chemical typically appears as a white crystalline solid. It plays a pivotal role in various chemical processes, particularly in the selective introduction of fluorine atoms into organic molecules. The reagent is especially valuable for monofluorination reactions targeting electron-rich double bonds, enol silyl ethers, and lithium enol salts.</p><p>
