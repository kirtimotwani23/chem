</p><p><strong>Industrial Grade 3-Methoxypropylamine (3-MPA)</strong> is a clear, colorless liquid that typically carries an ammonia-like odor. As a primary amine, it is characterized by high reactivity, making it a valuable intermediate in various chemical syntheses. Industrial Grade 3-MPA is fully miscible with water and a wide range of organic solvents, including ethanol, acetone, toluene, and hexane. It is primarily used in the production of active pharmaceutical ingredients, agricultural chemicals, water treatment products, and surfactants. Its chemical formula is C4H11NO, and it is widely recognized for its stability and effectiveness as a chemical intermediate.</p><p>
