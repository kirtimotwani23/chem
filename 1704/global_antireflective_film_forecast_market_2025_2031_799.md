</p><p><strong>Anti-reflective Film</strong> is a type of optical film applied to the front surface of displays to reduce light reflection and improve visual clarity. It is widely used in devices requiring enhanced display readability and aesthetic quality. These films often come with additional features such as:</p><p>
</p><p><strong>Antireflection</strong></p><p><strong>Antistatic performance</strong></p><p><strong>High durability</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Different types of anti-reflective films, such as <strong>Low Reflection (LR)</strong> and <strong>Anti-glare (AG)</strong> films, are tailored to meet specific application requirements across various industries.</p><p>
