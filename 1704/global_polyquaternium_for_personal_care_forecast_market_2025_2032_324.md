<h1>Polyquaternium for Personal Care Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Polyquaterniums are a class of synthetic polymers widely used in the personal care industry due to their cationic nature, which allows them to bond effectively with negatively charged surfaces like hair and skin. These polymers are either derived from natural sources through chemical modification or synthesized from monomers.
