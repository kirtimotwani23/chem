Unlike conventional adhesives that require solvents or curing agents, HMPSAs are applied in a molten state and form an instant bond upon cooling. These adhesives are widely used in packaging, labeling, tapes, medical applications, and automotive industries due to their excellent adhesion properties, durability, and ease of application.</p><p>
