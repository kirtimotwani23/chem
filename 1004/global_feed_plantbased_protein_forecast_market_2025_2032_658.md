Drivers:</p><p>
</p><p><strong>Growing Consumer Demand for Plant-Based Protein:</strong> The increasing consumer preference for plant-based diets, driven by health, environmental, and ethical considerations, is a major driver of the Feed Plant-based Protein Market. Restraints:</p><p>

The lack of awareness, taste preferences, and misconceptions about plant-based proteins can hinder market growth and adoption rates.</p><p><strong>Supply Chain Complexity:</strong> The complex supply chain of plant-based protein ingredients, including sourcing, processing, and distribution, can pose logistical challenges for manufacturers. Ensuring consistent quality, traceability, and sustainability of plant-based protein sources throughout the supply chain requires robust supply chain management practices.</p><p>
