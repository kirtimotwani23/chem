<strong>Market Size</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Polyurethane (PU) foam is a versatile polymer widely used in various industries due to its exceptional insulating properties, lightweight nature, and durability. It is available in two primary forms: rigid and flexible foam. Rigid PU foam is commonly used in construction and refrigeration, whereas flexible PU foam is extensively utilized in furniture, automotive, and packaging industries.</p><p>

	</p><p><strong>Growing Demand from the Construction Industry:</strong> PU foam is extensively used in insulation, roofing, and sealing applications, propelling market growth.</p><p><strong>Automotive Sector Expansion:</strong> Lightweight and energy-efficient foams improve vehicle performance and fuel efficiency.</p><p><strong>Rise in Consumer Goods Manufacturing:</strong> Mattresses, sofas, and packaging solutions drive the demand for flexible PU foam.</p><p><strong>Environmental Regulations Favoring Energy Efficiency:</strong> Increasing regulations for energy conservation boost PU foam adoption in buildings and appliances.</p><p>
