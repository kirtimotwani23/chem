<h1>Leaded Solder Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Leaded solder is a metallic alloy composed primarily of tin and lead, commonly used in electronic and industrial applications to create strong, conductive, and durable joints between metal surfaces. It is widely utilized in the electronics industry for circuit board assembly due to its lower melting point, ease of use, and reliability. Despite increasing regulations on lead usage, leaded solder remains a preferred choice for specific applications requiring superior mechanical and electrical properties.</p><p>
