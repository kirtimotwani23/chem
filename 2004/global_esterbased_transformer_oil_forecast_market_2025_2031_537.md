</p><p>These specialized transformer fluids are designed to perform two essential functions:</p><p>

</p><p>Ester-based Transformer Oil is a type of dielectric fluid formulated using either <strong>natural esters (typically derived from vegetable oils)</strong> or <strong>synthetic esters (engineered chemical compounds)</strong>. These oils are utilized in power and distribution transformers for their <strong>non-toxic, biodegradable nature</strong>, <strong>high flash and fire points</strong>, and <strong>superior moisture tolerance</strong> compared to conventional mineral oils. As the power infrastructure evolves towards sustainability and safety, ester-based transformer oils are increasingly favored in new installations and retrofitting applications.</p><p>
