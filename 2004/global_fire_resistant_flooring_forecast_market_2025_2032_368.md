</p><p><strong>Fire Resistant Flooring refers to flooring materials engineered with properties that resist ignition, inhibit flame spread, and reduce the production of toxic smoke. Specifically, fire resistant flooring combines the aesthetic appeal and functionality of conventional flooring—such as wood, vinyl, or laminate—with specialized treatments or composite materials that provide fireproof and flame-retardant qualities. These materials are crucial in enhancing the safety of buildings, particularly in commercial, industrial, and residential spaces where fire hazards pose significant risks.</strong></p><p>
</p><p><strong>Fire resistant flooring includes a wide range of solutions such as:</strong></p><p>
</p><p><strong>Fire-retardant engineered wooden floors</strong></p><p><strong>Flame-resistant vinyl composite tiles</strong></p><p><strong>Non-combustible ceramic or stone floorings with thermal insulation layers</strong></p><p><strong>Advanced laminate flooring with fire-resistant core boards</strong></p><p>
</p><p><strong>These floors are developed in compliance with global safety standards such as ASTM E648 (Critical Radiant Flux), EN 13501-1 (Euroclass fire classification), and others. By incorporating non-flammable materials or chemical treatments, they provide crucial time for evacuation during fire emergencies and minimize structural damage.</strong></p><p>
