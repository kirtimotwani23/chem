<h1>Ballistic Composite Material Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Ballistic composite materials are advanced engineered materials designed to provide high-impact resistance and superior protective capabilities against ballistic threats. These materials are widely used in defense, law enforcement, and civilian applications, including body armor, vehicle armor, and protective shields. Typically, ballistic composites are made from high-performance fibers such as aramid (Kevlar), ultra-high-molecular-weight polyethylene (UHMWPE), and advanced ceramics, which offer a balance of lightweight properties and high durability.</p><p>
