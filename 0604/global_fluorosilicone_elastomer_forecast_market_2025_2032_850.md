<strong>Definition</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Fluorosilicone elastomer (FVMQ) is a modified version of silicone rubber that has been fluorinated to improve its chemical resistance. This elastomer retains the advantageous properties of standard silicone, such as flexibility, temperature resistance, and weatherability, while offering enhanced resistance to fuels, oils, and solvents. Due to these properties, FVMQ is widely used in aerospace, automotive, and industrial applications where exposure to harsh chemicals and extreme temperatures is common.</p><p>
