<h1>Copper Extractant Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Copper extractants are chemical agents used in the solvent extraction process to separate and purify copper from ore. These extractants play a crucial role in hydrometallurgical copper production, facilitating the selective removal of copper ions from leach solutions. The process is widely used in mining and metallurgy, recycling, and wastewater treatment.</p><p>
