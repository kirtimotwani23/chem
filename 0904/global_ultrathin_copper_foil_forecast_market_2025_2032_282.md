<strong>Definition</strong></p><p>
</p><p>The Ultra-thin Copper Foil market refers to the copper foil produced through the electrolytic method by dissolving copper material in a copper sulfate solution. This copper foil, with a thickness of generally 2-5µm, is essential in batteries and electronic applications, especially with the increasing demand for copper foil thickness less than 5µm in the high-end market.</p><p>
