</p><p><strong>Boom in Telecommunication and Data Traffic</strong></p><p>

	The global shift toward <strong>5G technology</strong> demands low-latency, high-bandwidth networks—both of which rely heavily on advanced optical fibers. 	Hyperscale and edge data centers require high-speed interconnectivity, relying on fiber optic cables doped with germanium to enhance signal clarity and strength over long distances.</p><p><strong>Advancements in Fiber Optic Technologies</strong></p><p>

	The production of fiber-grade germanium is concentrated in a few countries, creating <strong>supply chain vulnerabilities</strong> and price volatility.</p><p>
<strong>Opportunities</strong></p><p>
</p><p><strong>Emerging Markets and Rural Connectivity Programs</strong></p><p>

	High-grade optical fibers with enhanced properties are gaining traction in <strong>aerospace communication and defense systems</strong>, where secure, high-speed data transfer is vital.</p><p>
