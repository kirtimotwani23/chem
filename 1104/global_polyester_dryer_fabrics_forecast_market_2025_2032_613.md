<h1>Polyester Dryer Fabrics Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Polyester dryer fabrics, also referred to as polyester dryer screens or conveyor belts, are essential components in papermaking and various industrial drying applications. These fabrics are typically manufactured using polyester monofilament or multifilament yarns, providing durability, heat resistance, and high permeability for effective moisture removal. They play a crucial role in the drying section of paper machines, aiding in the evaporation of water from paper sheets. Additionally, polyester dryer fabrics are used in industries such as food processing, textile manufacturing, and sludge dewatering.</p><p>
